Our mission

Ethnic Minorities of Burma and Resource Center (EMBARC) strives to help refugees expand their world of possibliites through advocacy, education and community development throughout Iowa.

How your DONATION Helps

Help us ensure our newest Iowans have access to food, shelter, clothing, education and health care.
  • $25 for a monthly refugee bus pass to get to school and work
  • $50 for blankets for a family of four during their first Iowa winter
  • $100 for a sewing machine to help earn income through the Community Fabric Project.
  • $250 for a new mattress for children sleeping on the floor.
